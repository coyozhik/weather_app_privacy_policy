Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Coyuzhik Weather ("we," "our," or "the App") handles information. Our App is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children.

1. Family and Children Friendly

Coyuzhik Weather is a general audience app and is appropriate for all ages. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from children under the age of 13, or any other users.

2. Information Collection and Use

To provide accurate weather updates, the App may use:

  • Location Data: The App accesses your location (precise or approximate) via your device's GPS or network to determine your current city and show relevant weather. This data is processed in real-time to fulfill your request. While we do not store this data on our servers for tracking or profiling, coordinates are transmitted to third-party providers solely to retrieve weather and location names (reverse geocoding).
  • Non-Personal Data: We may collect anonymous technical data (e.g., device model, OS version) to ensure the App works correctly on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party weather API providers to get forecast data. These providers receive only the geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) necessary to return weather information and do not receive any personal identifiers, names, or contact information.

4. Data Retention

We do not maintain a database of user locations or any personal profiles. Since no personal data is collected, no personal data is stored.

5. Permissions

The App requests only necessary permissions:

  • Location: To provide local weather.
  • Internet: To download weather forecasts.

6. Compliance

We ensure that our data practices are compliant with the Google Play Developer Distribution Agreement, including the Families Policy requirements.
